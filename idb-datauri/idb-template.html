Brand Guidelines

Logos

Our identity represents the commitment and purpose of our organization. By applying it consistently, we will increase awareness and strengthen our image.

Primary Logo
The primary logo is the preferred usage and should be applied in all contexts except for applications without sufficient contrast or reproduction capability.

Secondary Logo
The solid black logo should only be used in black and white applications. The solid white logo should be used over simple photgraphic backgrounds or when the primary logo does not offer sufficient contrast.

Clear Space
A minimum of "B" (the height of the "B" in the IDB Invest logo) area around the logo should be observed. These size requirements should be followed for all logo applications.

Minimum Size
The preferred minimum print size for the logo is 1 inch wide. The preferred minimum screen size is 100 pixles wide.

Color

Our color palette should always be bright, positive and bold. Orange (dominant color) is a critical brand signal that should never be overpowered. Orange and navy is the primary color palette we identify with. Secondary colors are best used for illustrating core sectors. They have been designed to support the primary brand palette.

Design Elements

Icon elements can be expanded into full compositions to enhance the meaning of each sector. Starting with the basic shpae-line segments, orientation, and gradient direction can be adjusted to communicate movement. The combination of mono-weight lines and bright shpaes should remian simple. These design elemnets should be prominent across all corporate communication material.
